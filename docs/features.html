Analytics

Monitor what's actually going on inside of your kiln. Optimize your schedules reducing time and cost.

Photo by Campaign Creators on Unsplash

Customizable

No matter what type of electric kiln you have, PyKiln can work with it. Flexible hardware and software design that scales to meet your needs.

Safety

PyKiln uses redundant relays and does self checks before and during each firing schedule. If a relay malfunctions it will stop the firing schedule and notify you of the problem.

Photo by Jalen Hueser on Unsplash

Free

PyKiln is an open source software and hardware project. The code, PCB designs, and bill of materials are all free to download, use, and modify. Collaborators and feedback are welcome!
